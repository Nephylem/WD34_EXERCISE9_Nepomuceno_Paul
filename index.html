    About Us

    Innovation has always been at the heart of our never-ending pursuit of loyal, happy Guests. Our chefs are constantly trying new dishes and refining old favorites. Much of our testing is done in live, working kitchens, allowing us to get immediate Guest feedback on what’s a new hit and what needs more work. As tastes and trends change, so do we; bringing not only new flavors and combinations to classic dishes but also fresh culinary inventions that delight and satisfy our biggest fans as well as our newest Guests.

    Of course none of this would matter if it weren’t combined with excellent service in a comfortable and fun atmosphere. Huge portions, hand-crafted drinks and a friendly, expertly-trained staff are all hallmarks of the Pasayan experience. From shore to dock, prep table to dining room table, the name Pasayan Resto has become synonymous with fresh, delicious seafood served in a fun, festive atmosphere that you can’t find anywhere else.
